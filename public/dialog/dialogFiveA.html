As she swings the lipstick in your direction you instinctively evade her first attack. While you twist the cap off of a bottle of water, the crazed woman sprays a cloud of intoxicating perfume around you. Coughing violently you throw the water in her face. With a deafening howl the saleswoman shrinks away from you. You faintly hear the sound of something falling. *clink clink*
“My, my foundation! It’s melting!” Her hands clasping her face, worsening the effect of the water on her caked layers of make up. Panda-eyed and defeated she throws you the ugliest look you have ever seen. In a series of sudden motions she claws her way out the store, leaving you still coughing and catching your breath.
On the floor, half hidden under a shelf, lies a key. You pick it up and make your way to the cash counter. Somewhere there must be a lock to match it. In a cabinet underneath the counter you find a hidden safe.
