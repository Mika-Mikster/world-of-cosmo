Unsure what to do next you pick up the bag and stare ahead for a while. The world you are in is your world, and yet it is not. Distressed you make a run for it, but as soon as you reach the end of the street you notice that to your left the cosmetics store is still there.
You’ve got no choice. Rather than enter with hung shoulders you raise your head and barge in. Aside from the soft buzzing of the lights you hear nothing. There don’t seem to be any people inside. You walk around…
